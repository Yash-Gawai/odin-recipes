This is for butter chicken!!

Butter chicken was originally made using the left over tandoori chicken meat from the previous night and as to not waste it, it was cooked with gravy and it gave birth to one of the best recipes (imo).

Ingredients:

  • 500 gm chicken (chopped in bite sized pieces)
  • 200 gm curd
  • 2 green chillies
  • 10 tablespoons of oil
  • 2 eggs

    • For the gravy:

  • 2 onions, diced
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 2 tablespoons cream

Steps:

  1. Marinate the chicken with curd, salt, pepper, garlic paste and chilli powder
  2. Leave it for 30 minutes atleast
  3. Heat up oil in the pan and add a spoon of butter. Add garlic and add diced onions. Add any spices according to taste and add tomatoes.
  4. Now let this cool. And heat up oil in a pan.
  5. Add chicken to it and let it cook perfectly and until it has a bit of charring
  6. While chicken is being cooked, grind the gravy and after the chicken has been cooked, add it to this gravy
  7. Let it simmer and add a bit of butter and when its time to serve, garnish it with cream
